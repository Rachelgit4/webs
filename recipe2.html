Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with cheese, vegetables and meat.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat eggs with salt and pepper. Add water/milk for fluffiness.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs and tilt pan so they spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Add fillings when eggs are slightly runny in the middle.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold one edge and let cook for a minute.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot.
